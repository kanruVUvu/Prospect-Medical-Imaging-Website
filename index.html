During the test
Ultrasound is a non-invasive examination that obtains images of the organs such as the liver, kidneys, the pelvis, the heart and major blood vessels. It involves high frequency sound waves, which are transmitted through the skin and reflected by the internal organs and structures in the body to provide information about various conditions. It is a pain free examination which is used to examine adults, children and babies..
